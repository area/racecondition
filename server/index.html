Race Condition
for Tildagon

Race Condition is a love letter to Spaceteam written through EMFCamp-tinted glasses. It is a cooperative game for 1-6 players, played on the Tildagon (and hexpansions). Each badge shows commands meant for someone else (unless playing alone). Calmly and clearly tell your teammates what to do, and successfully carry out as many commands as you can before time runs out.

For a slightly more in-depth explanation about where this came from and how it came to be, check the about page.

How to play

  1. 1 One player creates a room from their badge. Everyone else joins by selecting the same room number.
  2. 2 Each badge gets a different colour.
  3. 3 Your badge shows commands for other colours: "Green: press Button A on the Tildagon". Communicate that instruction to the correct player while everyone else is doing the same in, presumably, a calm and orderly fashion.
  4. 4 Simultaneously, carry out the command you're being told to by a teammate. Complete it and your teammate gets a new command.
  5. 5 Pass as many commands as possible before the 2-minute timer hits zero. More players and Hexpansions mean a higher score multiplier.

Commands and Hexpansions

Supported hexpansions plugged in to badges playing bring more variety of commands to the game - and the more you have, the bigger your score multiplier.

🔷 Tildagon 2024

Badge

The trusty old 2024 Tildagon. Press any of the six buttons around the edge — each shown as the arrow pointing at it — or shake or flip the whole badge (NB if any hexpansions are plugged in, 'Shake' and 'Flip' are disabled. There was... an incident during testing).

🕹️ MegaDrive

Hexpansion

A Sega MegaDrive controller hexpansion. 3-button pads give you the D-pad, diagonals, Start, A, B, C plus a couple of classic fighting-game combos. 6-button pads auto-unlock three more if one of the additional buttons is pressed.

START ↑ ↓ ← → ↖ ↗ ↙ ↘ A B C ↓ ↘ → A ↓ ↙ ← B X Y Z MODE
X · Y · Z auto-unlock on 6-button pads
MODE is permanently disabled due to a mysterious bug
