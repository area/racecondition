SpaceTeam
for Tildagon

A cooperative game for 2-6 players, played on the Tildagon (and hexpansions). Each badge shows commands meant for someone else. Calmly and clearly tell your teammates what to do, and successfully carry out as many commands as you can before time runs out.

How to play

  1. 1 One player creates a room from their badge. Everyone else joins by selecting the same room number.
  2. 2 Each badge gets a different colour.
  3. 3 Your badge shows commands for other colours: "Green: press Button A". Shout it at the right player.
  4. 4 Carry out the command you're told to by a teammate. Complete it and your teammate gets a new command.
  5. 5 Pass as many commands as possible before the 2-minute timer hits zero. More players and Hexpansions mean a higher score multiplier.

Commands and Hexpansions

Supported hexpansions plugged in to badges playing bring more variety of commands to the game - and the more you have, the bigger your score multiplier.

🔷 Tildagon 2024

Badge

The trusty old 2024 Tildagon. Press any of the six buttons around the edge, or shake the whole badge (NB if any hexpansions are plugged in, 'Shake' is disabled. There was... an incident during testing).

a b c d e f shake

🕹️ MegaDrive

Hexpansion

A Sega MegaDrive controller hexpansion. 3-button pads give you Start, A, B, C - 6-button pads auto-unlock four more if one of the additional buttons is pressed.

start a b c x y z mode
x · y · z · mode auto-unlock on 6-button pads
